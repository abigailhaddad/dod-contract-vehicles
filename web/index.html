About this data

Source: USASpending.gov bulk award archives for Department of Defense (agency code 097).

  • Ceiling = potential_total_value_of_award (includes all options)
  • Obligated = total_dollars_obligated (cumulative, latest transaction)
  • Ceiling Remaining = Ceiling - Obligated (approximate; does not reflect deobligations or ceiling modifications not yet in USASpending)
  • Active = current PoP end date is in the future
  • Expiring Soon = PoP end date within 6 months
  • Vehicle Rollup groups task/delivery orders by their parent IDIQ/BPA PIID. Ceiling and obligation totals are summed across all orders, not the vehicle-level ceiling itself.
  • Geographic coverage = primary place of performance (state), not necessarily the full geographic scope of the vehicle.
